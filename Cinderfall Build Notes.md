# Cinderfall — Build Notes

## Overview
A dark fortified manor built into a volcanic rock face. The structure should feel ancient and embedded — grown from the volcano rather than placed beside it.

---

## Dimensions

| Structure | Size |
|---|---|
| Keep (footprint) | 30 x 50 blocks |
| Outer walls | 60 x 60 blocks |
| Courtyard buffer | ~15 blocks on each side |

---

## Silhouette & Layout
- **Two flanking spires** at the short ends of the keep (fortified manor style)
- Keep partially carved into the volcanic rock face — natural rock forms the back wall
- Spires at roughly 1.5–2x keep wall height for visual hierarchy

---

## Block Palette (Vanilla)
- **Blackstone** — primary structure, smooth texture
- **Deepslate** — primary structure, rougher texture contrast
- **Magma blocks** — accent material; lava channels through walls and foundation (environmental storytelling — volcano proximity)
- **Obsidian** — trim and detail; represents hardened lava flows that became structural over time

### Additional ideas to explore
- Netherrack patches in courtyard floor (warm ground feel)
- Actual lava seeping through deepslate gaps in walls

---

## Setting
- Built beside / into a **volcano**
- Magma and obsidian are lore-justified, not just aesthetic
- Rock face integration on at least one side of the keep
