# The Hearthlands — World Overview

**Status:** 1.19.2 Test Build (temporary) → 1.20.1 Full Build (planned)

---

## World Premise

The Hearthlands is a vast medieval fantasy realm where players claim territories, build factions, and shape their own legends. The world is defined by natural wonders, ancient castles, dungeons, and mysteries waiting to be discovered and conquered.

---

## Core Systems

### Territory & Factions
- **Current System (1.19.2):** Cupboard mod for land protection (temporary)
- **Planned System (1.20.1):** Conquest Craft for faction-based territory claiming via Domain Blocks
- **Gameplay:** Factions compete for territory control; leaderboard based on conquest and expansion
- **Mechanics:** Players establish factions, claim chunks, defend territories, wage territorial wars

### Lore & Quests
- **System:** FTB Quests (lore-driven progression)
- **Phase 1:** Narrative-driven quests introducing world history, Cinderfall, and factions
- **Phase 2:** Gameplay-driven progression quests (mechanical unlocks, items, advancement)
- **NPCs:** Custom NPCs populate Cinderfall and provide quest exposition and rewards

### Music & Atmosphere
- **Medieval Music:** zmedievalmusic-1.19.2
- **Biome Music:** biomemusic-1.19.2
- **Atmosphere:** Creates immersive fantasy world through audio

---

## Key Landmarks & Points of Interest

### Cinderfall
- **Location:** Volcano biome (TBD exact placement)
- **Lore:** An ancient volcanic civilization that fell to ruin when the volcano erupted. Now stands as a haunting monument and place of power. Shrouded in mystery and danger.
- **Purpose:** 
  - Major world landmark and lore anchor
  - Faction base location
  - Raid/dungeon exploration site
  - NPC hub for lore exposition
- **Implementation:** Custom structures via Structurize (planned for 1.20.1)

### Castle Dungeons
- **Mod:** castle_dungeons-4.0.0
- **Purpose:** Procedurally generated dungeons with treasure and challenges
- **Player Use:** Raiding, exploration, resource gathering

### Castle Blocks & Architecture
- **Mod:** castleblocks-1.0.9.1
- **Purpose:** Medieval castle construction blocks and materials
- **Player Use:** Faction base building, settlement construction

### Waystones
- **Mod:** waystones-forge-1.19.2
- **Purpose:** Fast travel network for efficient world navigation
- **Lore:** Ancient waypoints scattered across the realm

## World Generation & Biomes

### Biomesoplenty
- **Total Biomes:** 80+ available; curated selection of ~18–20 fantasy-appropriate biomes enabled
- **Terrain Variety:** Mountains, forests, swamps, grasslands, crystalline caves
- **Aesthetic:** All non-fantasy/alien biomes disabled for cohesive medieval fantasy feel

### Terrain & Geography
- **Navigation:** Xaero's Minimap + Xaero's World Map for player exploration
- **Fast Travel:** Waystones for interconnected world movement
- **Exploration:** Encourages discovery of Biomesoplenty biomes and natural landmarks

- **Implementation:** TBD in Phase 2 quests

- **Lore Role:** Knights and warriors shape territorial control through skill and strength

- **Style:** Medieval with fantasy elements (castles, dungeons, magic, mystical artifacts)

- **Territory:** Factions claim chunks via Domain Blocks (Conquest Craft, 1.20.1)
- **Leadership:** Hierarchical roles (leaders, officers, members) TBD
- **Economy:** Factions earn currency but do NOT affect leaderboards

## Themes & Atmosphere

### Overall Tone
- **Welcoming:** Accessible to new players; clear onboarding through quests
- **Competitive:** Territorial warfare and faction conquest creates engagement
- **Mysterious:** World lore unfolds through exploration, NPCs, and quests
- **Community-Driven:** Factions encourage player cooperation and social bonds

### Visual Identity
- **Architecture:** Castle blocks, medieval dungeons, fortress construction
- **Biomes:** Fantasy forests, mystical caves, lush meadows, dark swamps
- **Audio:** Medieval and biome-specific music creates immersive atmosphere
- **Overall:** Cohesive medieval fantasy world without jarring modern elements

### Player Role
- **Conqueror:** Build faction power through territorial conquest
- **Builder:** Construct impressive castles and settlements
- **Explorer:** Discover dungeons, waystones, biomes, and lore
- **Adventurer:** Engage with world quests and NPC narratives
- **Social:** Form alliances, wage wars, build faction community
