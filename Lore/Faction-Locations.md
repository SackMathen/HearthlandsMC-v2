# Faction Locations
## Quick Reference Guide

A simple reference documenting where each major faction is headquartered in the realm.

---

## The Four Major Factions

### The Pathfinders' Guild
**Location:** Dawnmere  
**Coordinates:** 1417, 67, -5495  
**Biome:** Plains  
**Role:** Merchants, explorers, and traders. Serve as the central hub and crossroads of the realm.

---

### The Iron Crown
**Location:** Cinderfall  
**Coordinates:** -199, 110, -3173  
**Biome:** Volcanic/Mountain  
**Role:** Military power and industrial force. Control obsidian harvesting and forge production.

---

### The Deeproot Collective
**Location:** Whisperwood  
**Coordinates:** -1661, 71, -6051  
**Biome:** Ancient Forest  
**Role:** Nature guardians and druids. Commune with the earth and protect the natural world.

---

### The Archivists of Starlight
**Location:** The Verdant Reach  
**Coordinates:** 2149, 108, -4681  
**Biome:** Deep Forest  
**Role:** Scholars and knowledge keepers. Preserve and study the world's secrets.
