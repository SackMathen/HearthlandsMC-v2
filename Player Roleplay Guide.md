# Player Roleplay Guide

## Getting Started in The Hearthlands

### Creating Your Character
- What is your role in this world? (Knight? Merchant? Mage? Settler?)
- What is your character's background?
- What are you building toward? (A town? A kingdom? Mastering magic?)

---

## Common Roleplay Archetypes

### The Settler
- Focuses on building towns and communities
- Works with Towny to establish claims and manage resources
- Often becomes a town mayor or leader

### The Knight
- Combat-focused, uses Spartanweaponry and Immersive Armors
- Protects settlements or raids dungeons
- May form a military faction or house

### The Mage
- Uses Ars Nouveau spellcasting
- Seeks magical knowledge and artifacts
- Could establish a tower or magical academy

### The Merchant
- Trades goods and resources
- May establish trade routes between towns
- Uses economy system to build wealth
